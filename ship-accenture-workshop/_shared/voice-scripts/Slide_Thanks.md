## Spoken

Leave this up and don't rush off it.

## Professor

Consider what happens when a presenter rushes off the closing slide — they signal that the session is over and the relationship is transactional. The reason this matters is that the Thanks slide is not a formality; it is the last thing the room sees, and it sets the emotional tone for whatever conversation or follow-up comes next.

What this tells us is that your job here is simply to hold the space. Don't advance, don't look at your watch, don't start packing up. Let the room decompress and let any final questions surface naturally. Authoritative presenters are comfortable with the pause — it signals confidence, not dead air. Leave it up.
